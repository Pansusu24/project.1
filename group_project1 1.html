English

What is English?

IGCSE English helps students improve reading, writing and communication skills. Students learn how to understand texts, write clearly and express ideas with confidence.



What do students learn from it?

Students practise writing essays, stories and reports. They also learn grammar, vocabulary and how to analyse texts.



Why is it important to learn English?

English is useful in every subject and helps students succeed in school and in future careers.

Mathemathic

What is Mathematics?

Mathematics teaches students how to solve problems using numbers, formulas and logical thinking.



What do students learn from it?

Students study topics like algebra, geometry, fractions, graphs and statistics. They also learn how to apply maths to real-life situations.



Why is it important to learn Mathematic?

maths helps students think clearly and is useful for science, business and many careers.

Science

What is Science?

IGCSE Science helps students understand how the world works through experiments and scientific ideas.



What do students learn from it?

Students study Biology, Chemistry and Physics. They learn about the human body, energy, matter and the environment.



Why is it important to learn Science?

Science helps students develop curiosity and problem-solving skills that are useful in everyday life.

Business Studies

What is Business Studies?

IGCSE Business Studies teaches students how businesses work and how companies make decisions.



What do students learn from it?

Students learn about marketing, finance, customers and how to manage a business. They also study how businesses grow and succeed.



Why is it important to learn Business Studies?

It helps students understand the business world and is useful for future careers in business or management.

Economics

What is Economics?

IGCSE Economics teaches students how money, jobs and businesses affect everyday life.



What do students learn from it?

Students study supply and demand, prices, trade and how governments manage the economy.



Why is it important to learn Economics?

Economics helps students understand how the world works and how financial decisions are made.

Accounting

What is Accounting?

IGCSE Accounting teaches students how to record and manage financial information.



What do students learn from it?

Students learn how to prepare accounts, track income and expenses and understand profit and loss.



Why is it important to learn Accounting?

Accounting is important for business and helps students develop strong organisational skills.

ICT

What is ICT?

IGCSE ICT teaches students how to use technology safely and effectively.



What do students learn from it?

Students learn how to use computers, create documents, work with data and understand how digital systems work.



Why is it important to learn ICt?

ICT skills are needed in almost every job and help students stay confident in a digital world.

Computer Science

What is Computer Science?

IGCSE Computer Science teaches students how computers work and how software is created. It helps students understand technology in a deeper way.



What do students learn from it?

Students learn programming, problem-solving and how data is stored and processed. They also study topics like algorithms, computer systems and cybersecurity basics.



Why is it important to learn Computer Science?

Computer Science helps students develop logical thinking skills and prepares them for future careers in technology and programming.
